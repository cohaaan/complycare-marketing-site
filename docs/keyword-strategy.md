## Competitor Content Gap Analysis

### vs. MAXMRJ

- MAXMRJ focuses narrowly on discharge and readmission reduction
- They do NOT cover: compliance calendar automation, admissions accountability, end-of-day command reporting, or full-facility role accountability
- **Gap to exploit:** Full-facility operational OS positioning. Blog around "beyond discharge — why SNFs need end-to-end workflow accountability."
- MAXMRJ targets hospital discharge planners. ComplyCare targets the facility administrator/LNHA. Different buyer, different content angle.

### vs. Radial

- Radial focuses on discharge workflow and value-based care performance
- They do NOT cover: recurring compliance automation, audit defensibility, or admissions agreement tracking
- **Gap to exploit:** Compliance and survey readiness content. Radial has no footprint here. Owning "SNF compliance calendar" and "survey preparation software" in search is wide open.
- Radial is investor-facing heavy. ComplyCare can win with practitioner-voice content (written for LNHAs, DONs, and admissions directors).
